Your New Home Away From Home !

There's a seat at the table for everyone at Susan's Restaurant. We started with a simple goal:to create a space where the community could gather, relax and enjoy a truly great meal.

Whether you're here for a quick lunch, a Sunday celebrati, or just a coffee and a quiet moment, we aim to make you feel like family. Our menu is built on comfort, using recipes that feel familiar but taste extraordinary. Pull up a chair-the door is always open.
